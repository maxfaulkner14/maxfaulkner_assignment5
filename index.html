ABOUT US

Located in The Summit at Fritz Farm, Piazza Duomo is a purveyor of fine Italian dining...

PIAZZA DUOMO OPENED IN JANUARY 2018 AND IS LOCATED INSIDE THE SUMMIT AT FRITZ FARM. SINCE THEN, IT HAS BEEN SERVING BOTH QUALITY INTERPRETATIONS OF CLASSIC DISHES AND DARING NEW VENTURES INTO THE UNCHARTED TERRITORIES OF FOOD

PIAZZA DUOMO'S INTERIOR, REFLECTS THE HIP SENSIBILITIES OF THE SURROUNDING AREA WHILE ALSO PAYING TRIBUTE TO ITALY'S STORIED PAST. THE MULTI-LEVEL RESTAURANT OFFERS THE CHOICE TO OBSERVE AND RELAX AT THE CASCADE BAR. ALSO WATCH THE ART OF COOKING AS IT OCCURS IN THE OPEN-KITCHEN DINING ROOM
